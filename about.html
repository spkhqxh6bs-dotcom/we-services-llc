About We Services LLC

Slogan: “We make things happen”

Promise: “Anything you need we can provide”

Promise Slogan: “In due time”

WESERVICES LLC was founded unofficially in 2018 and became official in 2022. WESERVICES LLC was built to become the #1 consulting firm that prides themselves on customer service, client loyalty, efficiency, and solution oriented standards. Willie & Jazemine Evans have grown the company since the start and have expanded its areas of expertise. Now they look to build a strong consulting company on areas that clients need advice or more improvement on to better enhance their business. WESERVICES LLC prides themselves on “Making Things Happen” and improving all communities one at a time.

Leadership

Jazmine Evans

Jazemine Evans, is a proud native of Suffolk, VA and a graduate of Virginia State University with a Bachelor's Degree in Criminal Justice. As an African American woman she is committed to service, spending most of her time dedicating it to empowering minority individuals, uplifting communities, and coordinating meaningful events. My mission is to make a lasting impact by combining education, leadership, and purpose driven event design and decor that brings individuals dreams to a reality.

Willie Evans

Willie Evans, is a proud native of Rochester, NY and a graduate of Hampton University with a Bachelor's Degree in Criminal Justice. As an African American Male he is committed to building connections, spending most of his time mentoring youth and young men, while uplifting communities, and coordinating meaningful events. His mission is to make a lasting positive impact on all customers and build a team of professionals that make the lives of others easier with the right information.
